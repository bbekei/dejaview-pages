How it works

Four steps to photo peace

01

Scan

DejaView walks your folders and generates a pixel-level fingerprint for each image. Everything stays on your machine — nothing is uploaded.

02

Group

Identical and similar images are grouped together. You can see exactly how many copies live in which folders.

03

Decide

Mark files manually or use smart auto-selection: keep the oldest, the newest, or the highest-resolution copy.

04

Clean

Selected duplicates move to a soft-delete bin with 30-day recovery. An independent safety check runs before anything is removed.

Privacy & safety

Your photos stay yours

DejaView works with fingerprints, never the photos themselves. All processing happens locally on your computer — nothing is uploaded anywhere unless you choose to share your fingerprint file with family.

🔒
100% local processing Your photos never leave your machine. The scanner only generates fingerprints — it never moves or copies the originals.
🗂
Only fingerprints sync When you share with family, only the hash database goes to Google Drive — never the actual photos.
↩
30-day undo window No photo is permanently deleted without your explicit approval. Anything in the bin can be restored.
✅
Independent safety check A separate verification module double-checks that no photo you want to keep ends up in the bin.

Features

Everything you need to tame your library

🔍

Duplicate Detection

Pixel-accurate fingerprint matching: if two images are the same, DejaView finds them — regardless of folder, filename, or date.

🌊

Similarity Grouping

That burst of 40 nearly identical shots? Grouped together so you can pick your favourites and let go of the rest.

🤖

Smart Auto-Selection

Set a rule — "keep the highest resolution" or "keep the oldest" — and the app does the picking for you.

🗑

Safe Bin

30-day grace period, reversible decisions, and an independent module that verifies everything before removal.

🔗

Family Sync

Share fingerprint databases over Google Drive. The whole family can discover gaps in their shared photo heritage.

⚡

Fast & Resumable

Multi-threaded scanning with pause and resume. Close the app mid-scan and pick up exactly where you left off.

System requirements

What you need to run DejaView

💻

Operating System

Windows 10 or Windows 11

💾

Disk Space

~150 MB for the app. Thumbnails and database use roughly 150 MB per 10,000 images.

🧠

RAM

4 GB minimum, 8 GB recommended for large libraries

☁

Google Drive (optional)

Required only for family sharing. A free Google account is enough.

🌐

Network

Not required. All scanning and deduplication works fully offline.
